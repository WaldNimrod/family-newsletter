# Editor Kit — Tzlil's editorial Gem (Google Gemini, free)

Sets up **Tzlil** (Editor-in-Chief) with a **free Gemini "Gem"** on her own Google account,
so she edits **בית ולד 📰** from **her own computer** — no paid license — and **learns to
work with context / instructions / projects** along the way (a real skill, a sub-goal of its
own).

## Setup (do it *with* her — it IS the lesson)
1. Tzlil signs in at **gemini.google.com** with her own Google account.
2. **Gems → New Gem** → name it **"עורכת בית ולד"**.
3. Paste **GEM_INSTRUCTIONS.md** into the Gem's *Instructions* field.
4. (Weekly) add the week's **draft** as knowledge/context.
5. Kid-friendly walkthrough for her: **SETUP_FOR_TZLIL.md**.

## Bonus draw — Nano Banana 🍌
Gemini's image generation (Nano Banana) is right there. Tzlil can play, and even help draw
**Skipper Cat** (contribute poses to the MEDIA_KIT). A great creative hook — and teaches her
image-prompting too.

## Cost
**Start on her free Google account.** Upgrade to a minimal paid tier **only if** we hit a real
limit. (Edition #1 = keep it simple; she's getting to know the basic tool.)
