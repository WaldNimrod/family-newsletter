# HERO PROMPT — the flagship cover / teaser image (most important asset)

Fully self-contained (works with or without the bible in context). Generate the
landscape variant for the newsletter cover and/or the portrait variant for the WhatsApp
teaser — keep everything else identical.

```
A single flagship cover illustration for a warm family newsletter, in a hand-drawn children's-book comic style — "Quentin Blake meets Hergé": lively, slightly scratchy black-ink linework with clean FLAT "ligne claire" color fills. Bold, confident, slightly uneven black ink outlines (#2c2c2c) of varying weight. FLAT solid colors ONLY — absolutely NO smooth gradients, NO soft shading, NO airbrush, NO 3D, NO photorealism, NO glossy highlights. Where depth is needed use flat offset shadow shapes with ZERO blur (comic style). Bright cheerful morning light, warm and inviting.

HERO CHARACTER (centerpiece, lower-center, ~40% of the frame): "Skipper Cat" — a friendly cartoon domestic cat standing upright on two legs, front three-quarter view. Fur WHITE (#ffffff) with a few warm-red (#c0392b) stripe accents on the tail. He wears a TALL stovepipe hat with bold horizontal RED (#c0392b) and WHITE stripes (Cat-in-the-Hat style). Large round friendly eyes looking toward the viewer, small pink triangular nose, a few whiskers, a warm open smile. Cute child-friendly proportions: big head, small rounded body, little rounded paws. He stands at a large brown wooden ship's wheel (#8B4513, visible spokes and handles), holding it with one paw and raising the other paw in a friendly welcoming WAVE. Joyful, confident little-captain energy.

SCENE (flat, layered, simple — supports the character, never clutters):
- SKY (upper ~55%): bright blue sky in two or three FLAT horizontal color bands (deeper #2471a3 at the very top → lighter sky-blue lower; flat bands, NOT a gradient). A round friendly YELLOW sun (#f39c12) in an upper corner. Two or three simple rounded WHITE clouds with clean ink outlines.
- A cheerful RED diamond KITE (#e74c3c) with a little tail flying high, string trailing down.
- SEA (lower ~30%): flat blue water with simple stylized white-capped WAVES (clean ink curves, flat white caps). One small distant SAILBOAT on the horizon (white triangular sails). One or two playful cartoon FISH leaping mid-arc from the water with tiny splash marks.
- FOREGROUND: the wooden ship's wheel and a hint of a boat deck/railing, so the cat is clearly captaining the scene.

COMPOSITION & TEXT SPACE: character grounded lower-center; keep the UPPER sky OPEN and uncluttered as negative space for a headline/date to be overlaid later. Draw NO text, letters, words, numbers, speech bubbles, or logos anywhere. Balanced storybook composition with a comfortable margin (nothing important near the edges).

PALETTE — use ONLY these hex: ink #2c2c2c · white #ffffff · red #c0392b · wood #8B4513 · sky blue #2471a3 (+ a lighter tint) · sun yellow #f39c12 · kite red #e74c3c · warm cream #fdf6e3 for incidental warm areas. No other colors.

MOOD: warm, playful, adventurous, family-friendly — a beloved children's-book cover.

OUTPUT: high resolution, crisp clean edges, opaque.
— Newsletter cover variant: landscape 16:9, at least 1600×900.
— WhatsApp teaser variant: portrait 4:5, at least 1080×1350, with the open sky/text-space at the TOP and the cat lower in the frame.
```

**Tips:** generate 3–4 variations and pick the best silhouette/expression; then use that
image as the reference anchor for all the small poses. Save as `assets/hero/hero-scene.png`
(landscape) and, if you make the portrait teaser version, `assets/hero/hero-teaser.png`.
